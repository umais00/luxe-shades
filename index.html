FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Can I see samples before making a decision?

Absolutely! We offer free in-home consultations where you can view and feel different samples. Our experts will bring a selection of options for you to choose from, ensuring you find the perfect match for your home.

Are your blinds safe for children?

Yes, the safety of our customers, especially children, is our top priority. Our blinds are designed and manufactured to comply with safety standards and regulations. We offer various child-safe options, such as cordless blinds, motorized blinds, and wand-operated blinds, to eliminate the risk of entanglement.

How do I keep the blinds clean and well maintained?

Cleaning and maintaining our blinds is easy. The cleaning instructions may vary depending on the type of blind and material. Generally, regular dusting or vacuuming with a soft brush attachment is enough to keep the blinds clean. For stains or stubborn dirt, spot cleaning or gentle wiping with a damp cloth might be needed. Our team will provide you with detailed care instructions specific to the blinds you choose.

What kind of warranty do your blinds come with?

We stand by the quality of our products and offer a warranty on our blinds. The specific warranty terms may vary depending on the type of blind and components. Our team will provide you with the warranty details and answer any questions during the consultation. You can rest assured that we aim to provide durable and long-lasting blinds for your satisfaction.

Can I get custom blinds for my windows?

Absolutely! We understand that every window is unique, so we offer custom-made blinds. During our free measurement and consultation service, our experts will take accurate measurements and help you choose the perfect size and fit for your windows. Your blinds will be customized to meet your specific requirements.

Do you provide installation services?

Yes, we offer professional installation services to ensure your blinds are installed correctly and operate smoothly. Our team of experts will handle the entire installation process, giving you peace of mind and a hassle-free experience.

Feeling Overwhelmed? We Understand.

With so many options and conflicting information, it’s easy to feel unsure about where to start. At LuxeShades, we get it. That’s why we offer a quick, free, and easy consultation service to help you find the perfect blinds for your home. Let our experts guide you through the process with reliable advice and personalized recommendations.
